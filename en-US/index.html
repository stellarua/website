Exceeding expectations

Charitable organization “Stellar Ukraine” aims to help Kharkiv and other Ukrainian cities that are currently suffering from the war.

6 days a week, our mobile medical units go to different locations so that residents of the Kharkiv region have access to free high-quality medical and psychological care, as well as to the necessary medicines provided by partners.

  • Create opportunities

    to provide free quality medical care to people who need it

  • Solving

    urgent issues for the local and vulnerable population of the de-occupied territories, internally displaced persons (IDPs), as well as other needy groups

  • Beside you despite everything

    we provide assistance where others cannot

  • Closer than you think

    we work in hard-to-reach areas of the Kharkiv region, where there are no other opportunities to receive help
